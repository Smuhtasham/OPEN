About Us

OPEN is a non-profit, non-political, and non-religious organization that connects Oromo professionals and entrepreneurs from various fields, industries, experience levels, and backgrounds to provide opportunities for networking, professional development and personal growth.

Key Points

Inclusive Community: We welcome Oromo professionals and
entrepreneurs from all
Networking Hub: Connect with like-minded individuals,
fostering collaboration and innovation.
Professional Development: Access workshops and mentorship
programs tailored to your growth.
